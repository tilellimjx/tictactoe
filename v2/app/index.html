Help / Instructions

Get three of your symbols in a row (across, down, or diagonally) to win.

  • Click, tap, or use number keys 1–9 to place a symbol (top-left = 1, bottom-right = 9).
  • Easy AI plays randomly. Medium uses simple heuristics. Hard uses Minimax and is unbeatable.
  • Undo: Press the Undo button or Ctrl+Z / Cmd+Z to take back the last move (in HvAI mode, both AI's and your last move are reverted).
  • Sound: Toggle the speaker icon in the top bar. Sound is muted by default.
  • Themes: Pick Beach, Mountains, or Desert at the top — applied immediately.
  • Stats Dashboard: Shows wins, losses, draws, win-rate %, current streak, best streak, and head-to-head records.
  • Forfeit: Press the Forfeit button or Esc. Forfeits are not recorded as wins or losses.
