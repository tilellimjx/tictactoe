    Help / Instructions

    Get three of your symbols in a row (across, down, or diagonally) to win.

    • Click, tap, or use arrow keys + Enter to place a symbol.
    • Easy AI plays randomly. Medium uses simple heuristics. Hard uses Minimax and is unbeatable.
    • Undo / Redo within a round (Ctrl/Cmd+Z, Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Z).
    • Match Mode: Best-of-N rounds chosen on the menu.
    • Sound: Toggle the speaker icon to mute/unmute.
    • Themes: Pick Beach, Mountains, or Desert at the top.
